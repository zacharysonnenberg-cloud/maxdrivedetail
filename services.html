Policies

Clear Rules.
No Surprises.

These policies exist to protect your time and ours. If you have a question not covered here, just text Zach.
Booking Deposit

15% of the total estimate is due at booking to hold your slot. Full refund with 48+ hours notice. 50% kept for 24–48 hr cancellations. Deposit kept for under 24 hr cancellations.

Travel Fee

Free within 6 miles of Vienna, VA. 6–15 mi: +$15. 15–25 mi: +$25. 25+ miles: by quote. Not sure if you're in range? Text Zach.

Satisfaction Guarantee

Not happy with the result? Text Zach within 48 hours and he'll return to fix it at no charge. No refunds on completed labor, but the job isn't done until you're satisfied.

Payment

Zelle, Venmo, or cash — due on completion. Deposit uses the same methods. Minimum job: $100. No credit cards at this time.

Referral Program

Refer a friend and you both win: you get $20 off your next booking, and the new client gets $20 off their first. Mention the referral at booking — no codes needed.
